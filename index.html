Jumia-Tech: Covid 19 In-store Pick-up and Tech Suport Process Mapping

Jumia-Tech, a large supplier of consumer hardware devices such as laptops, tablets, printers, monitors, and cables needed a seamless pick-up process that adhered to Covid 19 Protocol while giving customers quality service. This process flow was designed to resolve Jumia-Tech's online purchases-in-store pickup process and their tech support process in response to the pandemic. The flow maps represent the TO-BE online purchase and in-store pickup and TO-BE tech support process in line with Covid 19 protocol. Flowmaps were designed using draw.io.
